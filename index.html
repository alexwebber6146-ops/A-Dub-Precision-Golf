About Alex Webber

PGA of Canada, Apprentice Professional Alex joined the PGA of Canada in 2024, after teaching the First Tee Program for 2 years, where he found his passion to teach. Alex has played golf for more than 20 years and is currently a playing professional on the Vancouver Golf Tour. He has been working at MGLA since 2022 and looks forward to continuing his connections with our avid golfers that choose to improve their game at MGLA. Message from Alex: Whether you are a beginner looking to start playing, or an advanced golfer looking to fine tune your game, I would love to work with you in a stress free, relaxed environment. The game of golf has been a major part of my life and I hope I can make it a major part of yours as well!
